Privacy

At Food Fusion, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. We collect essential data such as your name, contact details, delivery address, and payment information to process your orders and enhance your experience. Additionally, non-personal data like browsing behavior and device details may be collected through cookies to improve our website's functionality. Rest assured, your information is used solely for order fulfillment, personalization, and compliance with legal requirements, and we only share it with trusted partners like payment processors and delivery services.

We implement robust security measures to safeguard your data and never sell or rent it to third parties. You have the right to access, update, or delete your information and can opt out of promotional communications at any time. For inquiries or assistance, please contact us at support@fusionbite.com. Your trust is our priority, and we are dedicated to providing a secure and seamless experience.
